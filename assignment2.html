Interactive Slideshow

To view your slides:

  • Click the Right or Left arrow buttons to move forward or backward through the image list.
  • Click the Play/Pause button to automatically move forward through the image list.
  • Click an image to view it in full screen mode.

To add to favourites:

  • Click on the heart when you are viewing a photo you would like to add to favourites.
  • To remove from favourites, click on the photo in "My Favourites" and click on the red "Delete from favourites" button.
